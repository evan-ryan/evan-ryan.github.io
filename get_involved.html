EVENTS

Hiking Events Year-Round

At Great Hikes NE we are constatnly holding different types of events to keep the community engaged and involved. Some of these events include barbeques, camping, stargazing, and hiking seminars. Look out for our yearly Fourth of July Hike Weekend! We pride ourselves on having a full yearly schedule so there are always opportunities to get involved. Any season, rain or shine, there is always montly events that anyone is welcome to attend. Keeping an active community of like minded hikers is what we strive for a Great Hikes NE. As we grow larger we will be looking to set up travel trips where we can head outside the New England region for some good old fashion hiking roadtrips.

All ideas are welcome here at Great Hikes NE! Whether you just joined our community or have been a long term memember, we encourage any new ideas at all times. Have a great idea for a new montly event? Let us know! We are always looking for ways to improve the community and great new ideas are always a plus. If you are browsing our event calendar and do not see a fishing trip, go ahead and create the event! At Great Hikes NE anyone can be an event organizer. Make sure to reach out with any and all ideas!

Our Staff is Always here to help, feel free to reach out at any time!

COMMUNITY IS KEY

Fourth of July Hike Weekend

Here are some photos from last year's Fourth of July Hike Weekend
Use the scrolls arrows on the sides.


Since 2017 Great Hikes NE has been hosting an annual Fourth of July hiking weekend at Mount Kearsarge in Warren NH. Each year a large group of us bring out the barbaque grills, fireworks, and more food than anyone can eat. We have a blast each year with plently of family fun, as well as bonfires at night. We usually go up on a friday and camp up there for 2 days. After a weekend of fun we all head home on Sunday. This is a summertime blast you surely do not want to miss out on.

TRAINING SEMINARS

Training Sessions

As a way to further increase our hiking communities skills we have begun to host weekly training seminars and hikes. We will be on the trail every weekend 9 months out of the year. We take December, January, and February off as the weather is not too friendly to hikers at this time. Other than those 3 months we hold weekly hiking groups for all skills(weather permitting). To attend an event just reach out to the event supervisor for that day and they can provide details of the meeting spot and trail for the day.
