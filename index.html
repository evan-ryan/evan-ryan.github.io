Hiking Tips

One of most important things when it comes to hiking in the great outdoors is packing accordingly. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen and bug spray
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light

Lift Saftey

We must remember to always practice lift safety. Here are a few ground rules:

  1. Keep your hands inside the lift at all times
  2. Do not lean over the lift rails
  3. No rocking the lifts
  4. Do not throw or drop anything off the lift
