  1. Great Team Building Activity
  2. Awesome Exercise

Hiking Tips

One of most important things when it comes to hiking in the great outdoors is packing accordingly. At Great Hikes NE we offer many tips a guides on hiking. Here are a few tips:

  1. Bring layers of clothing
  2. Pack sunscreen and bug spray
  3. Carry extra water just in case
  4. Pack light.
