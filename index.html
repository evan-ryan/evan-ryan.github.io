ABOUT US

Hiking is our passion

Welcome to Great Hikes NE, the home of all things hiking in New England. The New England region is home to some of the best mountains and hiking trails in all of the United States. From experts to beginners, everyone can find their perfect trail in New England. Not only are there great trails but also some of the best nature around. Specifically, in the fall when the leaves begin to change colors you will be rewarded with some of the most breathtaking views of foliage the area has to offer.

Evan Ryan


I orginally founded great hikes as a way to find more people to share my passion of hiking with. The website quickly grew into a great online hiking community. From learning new trails to sharing my experience with others the website has become much more than what it started as. I look to continue to actively contribute to the growing hiking community this website offers. Hopefully we can continue to grow much bigger into the future. We are always welcoming new hikers from around the world!
