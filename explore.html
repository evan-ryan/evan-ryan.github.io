Mount Washington
White Moutains

The Highest Peak in the Northeastern United States

Mount Washington can be a challenge for even the most seasoned hikers, but make no doubt this is the pinnacle of the White Mountains!

Interesting Facts

Mount Washington is a very unique and spectacular mountain located in the northern part of New Hampshire, here are a few facts about the awesome mountain.

  • Peaks at 6,288.2ft
  • East of the Mississippi River, Mount Washington is the most topographically promiment mountain.
  • Called Agiocochook by some Native American tribes

Saftey Tips

Mount Washington is one of the most dangerous mountains in the Northeast. Make sure to follow saftey precautions. Here are some tips:

  • Weather on Mount Washington can be extremely erratic, make sure to pack clothes for the cold, even in the summer!
  • Turn back if conditions become too dangerous.
  • Make sure to know the route you are taking and study the trails.
  • Do not hike alone! Make sure to go with a partner or a group.
